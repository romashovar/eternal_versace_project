Chronological order

Here you will explore the exhibition in chronological order. This exhibition traces the history of Versace from its founding in 1978 by Gianni Versace to its evolution into a global symbol of Italian luxury. Bringing together archival garments, accessories, photographs, and multimedia materials, it explores how the house redefined glamour through bold color, classical references, and fearless sensuality. From the supermodel era of the 1990s to contemporary reinterpretations under new creative directions, Versace has continuously shaped the dialogue between fashion, celebrity, and popular culture. This exhibition invites visitors to discover how a family-founded atelier from Milan became one of the most influential fashion houses of the late twentieth and early twenty-first centuries.
