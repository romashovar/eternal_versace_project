Versace Medusa Aevitas Satin Shoes, 2021

1 / 3

These shoes are super tall and shiny, almost like wearing stilts! They have the Medusa face on them and make you feel like a star.

These are the Medusa Aevitas shoes, known for their huge platforms and high heels. Made of shiny satin and decorated with the Medusa emblem, they became a hit for their bold, glamorous look.

The Medusa Aevitas platform pumps epitomize Versace’s 21st-century maximalism. Featuring towering block heels, double platforms, satin finish, and an ankle strap adorned with the Medusa medallion, they reinterpret 1970s disco aesthetics for a contemporary audience. The exaggerated scale symbolizes empowerment through height and presence.
