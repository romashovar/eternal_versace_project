Elton John’s Suit, 1992

1 / 2

This is a shiny, bright costume made for Elton John, a famous singer who loves fun clothes. It’s like a superhero outfit for music!

Elton John wore Versace costumes on stage, and they were as colorful and bold as his music. This outfit shows sparkling details, bright colors, and playful shapes—perfect for his unforgettable concerts.

Versace’s collaboration with Elton John exemplifies the house’s affinity for theatricality and celebrity culture. Designed for stage performance, the costume combines ornate embroidery, bold color blocking, and sculptural tailoring, embodying the flamboyance of both the musician’s persona and Versace’s maximalist aesthetic. Such pieces function as both performance attire and cultural icons.
