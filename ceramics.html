Versace Tableware

These are plates and cups made by Versace. They are super fancy, with shiny gold and the Medusa head on them—like eating and sitting in a palace!

Versace doesn’t only make clothes—they also design furniture and tableware. Chairs, plates, and cups carry the same gold details and Medusa symbols you see in the clothes, bringing fashion into everyday life.

Versace Home, established in 1992, extended the brand’s aesthetic into lifestyle design. Characterized by neoclassical motifs, lavish ornamentation, and the iconic Medusa emblem, Versace’s furniture and porcelain collections embody the translation of luxury fashion codes into domestic space. They represent an early example of a couture house building a holistic lifestyle brand.
