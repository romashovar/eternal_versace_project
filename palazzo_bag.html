Palazzo Empire Bag, 2016

1 / 2

This bag has a Medusa face on it and looks very special. People carry it like treasure—it’s both pretty and useful.

The Palazzo Empire is one of Versace’s most famous handbags. Launched in 2016, it features the Medusa head on the front and is designed to look both elegant and practical.

Introduced in 2016, the Palazzo Empire handbag is defined by architectural structure, supple leather, and the central Medusa head plaque. Conceived as a versatile day-to-evening accessory, it reflects Donatella Versace’s emphasis on modern luxury and functionality, bridging heritage iconography with contemporary urban needs.
